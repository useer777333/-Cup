**☕ SwadCup – Edible Tea Cup**
_The last bite of your drink._

SwadCup is a Nepali startup idea that creates edible cups made from flour.
Customers can enjoy their tea and eat the cup afterward, reducing waste and promoting eco-friendly alternatives to plastic cups.

🌐 **Live Website**: https://swadcup.netlify.app/

Hosted on Netlify
Repository managed on GitHub

**🌱 About the Idea**

SwadCup introduces a sustainable solution for tea lovers.
Instead of throwing away disposable cups, the cup itself becomes part of the snack.

**Key Concept:**
Drink your tea → Finish your drink → Eat the cup

**✨ Features**
☕ Edible cup made from flour
🔥 Resists hot tea for 40–45 minutes
🍪 Crispy and tasty after drinking
🌍 Eco-friendly alternative to plastic cups
🇳🇵 Startup idea from Nepal
